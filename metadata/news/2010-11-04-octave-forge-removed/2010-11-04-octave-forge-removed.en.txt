The octave-forge packages were removed from the science overlay.
You will need to use app-portage/g-octave to install the octave-forge
stuff using your Gentoo package manager.

g-octave project page: http://www.g-octave.org/
g-octave documentation: http://doc.g-octave.org/latest/
